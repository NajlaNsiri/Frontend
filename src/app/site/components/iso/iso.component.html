Gold & PM Assaying

Gold & PM Assaying encompasses a series of crucial steps for the accurate analysis of precious metals like gold. Initially, samples are subjected to fusion and cupellation processes, which involve melting and separating the metal from impurities. Following this, digestion and dilution techniques are utilized to prepare the metals for analysis. Atomic Absorption methods are then employed to detect and measure the presence of gold and other metals with precision. Furthermore, the samples undergo separation via sieves before undergoing fire assay to determine the gold content accurately. These meticulous procedures ensure thorough assessment and quantification of precious metals for a variety of purposes.
